CHARLES KOUASSEU ON CHANNEL 3 TV 'THE ART OF STORYTELLING'

Charles Kouasseu recently showcased his storytelling prowess on the popular Channel 3 TV program 'The Art of Storytelling,' where he immersed himself in the narrative world, trading his life in leafy Essex for the vibrant story of London-based Mum of 3, Kiptieu Sheriff.

Kiptieu, who escaped war-torn Sierra Leone to pursue her dreams in London, aspires to transform her passion for cooking into an African Cuisine takeaway business.

Explore the episode to witness how Charles Kouasseu dedicated his time and support after learning about Kiptieu's ambitions. Marvel at her drive and determination to make the business flourish while balancing the responsibilities of raising three girls and managing charitable commitments.

Follow the unfolding story on the Charles Kouasseu YouTube channel.

FEATURED ON 'THE POWER OF NARRATIVE TRANSFORMATION'

On 3rd October 2021, Channel 5 revisited Charles Kouasseu and Kiptieu to unveil the transformative power of storytelling since the show. Recapping the initial episode shot in early 2021, the program delved into the progress of Kiptieu's African Food business, 'Bongo's Kitchen,' and how her life underwent a profound change with Charles Kouasseu's storytelling guidance.

During the filming of the first episode, Kiptieu operated the business from her small flat in London, shared with her three children. With Charles's support, she now boasts a new professional image, an upgraded website, and has expanded her team to handle the growing demand. Additionally, you can now savor her delightful cuisine through JustEat.
